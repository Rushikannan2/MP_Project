About the Graphical Method

The Graphical Method is a visual technique for solving linear programming problems with two variables by plotting constraints and identifying the optimal solution at the vertices of the feasible region.

Key Applications

  • Optimizing production plans in manufacturing
  • Maximizing profit in business operations
  • Minimizing costs in resource allocation
  • Solving diet optimization problems
