What is the Transportation Problem?

The Transportation Problem involves determining the most cost-effective way of transporting goods from multiple suppliers to multiple consumers while satisfying supply and demand constraints.

Applications of the Transportation Problem

  • Optimizing delivery routes for logistics companies.
  • Reducing transportation costs in supply chain management.
  • Maximizing resource distribution efficiency.
  • Cost minimization in transportation networks.
